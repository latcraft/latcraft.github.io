Growing Teams: Notes of an Amateur Team Gardener

Andrey Grave | BuyerDeck

Andrey will share his experience and discoveries on the topic of Growing Teams. What does it take to grow a team? What environment facilitates team growth? What distinguishes great teams? What can kill teams? What are characteristics of great team member? And some more.

R. Karpelcevs | Ask.fm

Having worked both as developer and as team manager, Aleksej will share his experiences with team, personal and process aspects that facilitate or inhibit productivity. About

Latvian Software Craftsmanship Community is a non-profit backed by the most active representatives of Latvian IT community. Our goal is to nurture top-notch software development culture in Latvia and raise the bar of professional software development by practicing it and helping others learn the craft by example. In pursuing of our goals, we organize recurring events dedicated to broad range of IT professionals who are eager to learn and share. Events run every first Tuesday in the month afterhours. Interactive and relevant high quality talks are inevitable part of the event â€“ IT Trends, Techies, Engineering Practices, Operations, Management, Leadership, all covered. Coffee breaks with snacks and drinks give an excellent chance to socialize and meet like-minded people.

Besides, our goal is to raise new speakers that will represent our country internationally by preparing, reviewing and giving the best advice. By inviting not only local participants, but also our friends from neighboring countries, we aspire to make it a significant event for Latvian IT, gathering about 100 monthly attendees including software architects, developers, quality assurance professionals, project managers and computer science students.
