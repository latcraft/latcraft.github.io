About

Latvian Software Craftsmanship Community is a non-profit backed by the most active representatives of Latvian IT community. Our goal is to nurture top-notch software development culture in Latvia and raise the bar of professional software development by practicing it and helping others learn the craft by example. In pursuing of our goals, we organize recurring events dedicated to broad range of IT professionals who are eager to learn and share. Events run every first Tuesday in the month afterhours. Interactive and relevant high quality talks are inevitable part of the event – IT Trends, Techies, Engineering Practices, Operations, Management, Leadership, all covered. Coffee breaks with snacks and drinks give an excellent chance to socialize and meet like-minded people.

Besides, our goal is to raise new speakers that will represent our country internationally by preparing, reviewing and giving the best advice. By inviting not only local participants, but also our friends from neighboring countries, we aspire to make it a significant event for Latvian IT, gathering 50-100 monthly attendees including software architects, developers, quality assurance professionals, project managers and computer science students.

Sponsorship

There are plenty of benefits of becoming a sponsor. Please contact us if you're interested in providing support.

Visibility

Get recognised within Baltic IT community as a company that cares about nurturing professional software development culture in the country. We’ll regularly spread a word about you.

Exposure

  • Pitch your company for 10 minutes
  • Award speakers on behalf of the company
  • Organize lottery or contest
  • Publish your logo on handouts, all presentations and community website
  • Place banner in the conference room right next to the speaker
  • Attract the best talent by placing open job positions on community website

Topic Suggestion

Suggest topic you’re especially interested in and we’ll do our best to invite professional with hands-on experience in this area to speak on one of the events.

Meeting the Best Talent

Get direct access to high profile industry leaders and people who are keen to learn and constantly maintain fresh view on software development.

Guaranteed Event and Conference Passes

Get guaranteed passes for 5 employees to any event even if all tickets are sold-out. In addition you'll get 5 free tickets to all conferences organized by the community during sponsorship year.

Training Discount

Get 15% discount for all trainings organized by the community during sponsorship year (e.g. Java Software Architecture in 2015).
